Safety

Introduction

Welcome to OpenAI's Safety Page, where our commitment to the responsible and safe development of artificial intelligence is at the forefront. As a leading AI research organization, we recognize the paramount importance of prioritizing safety measures to ensure the positive impact of AI on society. OpenAI is dedicated to fostering transparency in our practices and sharing our ongoing efforts to address the challenges associated with AI safety.

Developing Safe & Responsible AI

At OpenAI, our approach to developing safe and responsible AI is guided by a set of core principles and guidelines. We believe in continuous evaluation, adapting to emerging challenges, and seeking input from a diverse range of stakeholders. Our commitment to safety extends beyond the initial development stages, incorporating robust testing, error handling mechanisms, and regular audits to maintain the reliability of our AI systems.

Focus on Safety

The foundation of OpenAI's AI systems lies in the integration of robust safety features. Our dedication to ensuring safety is evident in our rigorous testing procedures, proactive error detection and handling, and a commitment to conducting regular audits. By placing a strong emphasis on safety, OpenAI aims to deliver AI technologies that not only push the boundaries of innovation but also prioritize the well-being of individuals and communities.
